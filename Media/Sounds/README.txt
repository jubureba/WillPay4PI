Will Pay 4 PI - Custom Sounds
==============================

Place custom .ogg sound files in this folder.
Reference them in the addon settings as:
  Interface\AddOns\PIAssistant\Media\Sounds\your_file.ogg

Requirements:
- Format: OGG Vorbis (.ogg)
- Sample rate: 44100 Hz recommended
- Channels: Mono or Stereo
- Duration: 0.5s - 3s recommended for alerts

The addon also supports WoW built-in sounds via the sound picker in settings.
